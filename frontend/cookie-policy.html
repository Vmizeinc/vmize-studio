2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and actions.

Cookies can be "persistent" (remain on your device until deleted or expired) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, authentication, and session management.

  • vmize_user - Stores user authentication and session information
  • vmize_access_token - JWT token for authenticated API requests
  • vmize_refresh_token - Refresh token for session renewal

3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the Website to remember choices you make (such as your login details) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

  • vmize_remember - Remembers "Remember Me" preference
  • vmize_visitor_id - Unique visitor identifier for session tracking
  • vmize_first_visit - Timestamp of first visit
  • vmize_nav_history - Navigation history for improved back button functionality

3.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • vmize_events - Tracks user interactions and page views
  • vmize_demo_tries - Tracks free demo usage count
  • vmize_demo_email - Email for extended demo access

3.4 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our Website to help us improve its performance.

  • Page load times and response times
  • Error tracking and debugging information
  • API performance metrics

7. Your Cookie Choices

Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • Delete all cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Allow only specific cookies
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

How to Manage Cookies by Browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Note: Blocking or deleting essential cookies may prevent you from accessing certain features of our Website or using our services effectively.

8. Local Storage

We use local storage for:

You can clear local storage data through your browser's developer tools or by clearing all browsing data.
