Getting Started

  • How do I create an account? ‚ñº
    Click the "Get Started" button on our homepage or go to the pricing page. Choose your plan (Starter, Professional, or Enterprise), fill in your details including company name and website, and you'll receive your API key immediately after registration.
  • What is a virtual try-on? ‚ñº
    Virtual try-on uses AI technology to show how clothing items look on a person. Customers upload a photo of themselves, and our AI generates a realistic image of them wearing your products. This helps customers make confident purchase decisions and reduces returns.
  • How long does setup take? ‚ñº
    Setup takes approximately 5 minutes. After creating your account, you'll receive your API key and integration code. Simply copy and paste the code into your website, and you're ready to go. For Shopify users, we offer a one-click installation.
  • Do I need technical knowledge? ‚ñº
    No technical knowledge is required. Our integration is designed to be simple copy-paste. If you can add a script to your website (like Google Analytics), you can integrate VMIZE. For custom implementations, our documentation and support team are available to help.

Integration

  • Which platforms are supported? ‚ñº
    VMIZE works with Shopify, WordPress, WooCommerce, and custom websites. We provide platform-specific integration guides and support for each. Our JavaScript widget works on any website that allows custom scripts.
  • Where do I find my API key? ‚ñº
    Your API key is available in your dashboard under Settings ‚Üí API Keys. It starts with "vmize_pk_live_" for production or "vmize_pk_test_" for testing. Never share your API key publicly.
  • Can I customize the try-on button? ‚ñº
    Yes! You can customize the button text, color, position, and styling through the widget configuration. See our integration guide for customization options.
  • How do I test before going live? ‚ñº
    Every account includes a test API key (vmize_pk_test_). Use this key on your staging site to test the integration before deploying to production. Test keys have the same features but don't count toward your credit usage.

Pricing & Billing

  • How does the credit system work? ‚ñº
    1 credit = 1 virtual try-on. Each plan includes monthly credits plus daily bonus credits that reset every 24 hours. Starter gets 300/month, Professional gets 1,000/month + 100 daily, Enterprise gets 3,000/month + 300 daily. Unused monthly credits don't roll over.
  • Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ‚ñº
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
  • What happens if I run out of credits? ‚ñº
    You can purchase top-up credits ($25 per 100 try-ons) or upgrade to a higher plan. We recommend upgrading as it offers better value. You'll receive notifications when you reach 80% and 90% of your monthly credits.
  • Do you offer annual plans? ‚ñº
    Yes! Annual plans save you 2 months (equivalent to ~17% discount). Starter: $490/year, Professional: $1,290/year, Enterprise: $2,990/year. Pay annually and save!

Troubleshooting

  • The try-on button isn't showing ‚ñº
    Check that: (1) The widget script is loaded on your page, (2) Your API key is correct, (3) The product ID is set correctly, (4) There are no JavaScript errors in the browser console. If issues persist, contact support with your website URL.
  • Try-on results are slow ‚ñº
    Virtual try-on generation typically takes 5-15 seconds. Factors affecting speed include image size, server load, and network connection. For best results, ensure images are under 5MB and in JPG or PNG format.
  • Getting API authentication errors ‚ñº
    Verify your API key is correct and starts with "vmize_pk_live_" (or "vmize_pk_test_" for testing). Check that your account is active and has available credits. API keys are case-sensitive.
  • Images not uploading ‚ñº
    Ensure images are: (1) Under 5MB in size, (2) In JPG, PNG, or WEBP format, (3) Clear and well-lit photos of people wearing clothes. Images must show a full torso view for best results.

API & Technical

  • What are the API rate limits? ‚ñº
    Rate limits depend on your plan: Starter (10 req/min), Professional (30 req/min), Enterprise (100 req/min). If you need higher limits, contact our sales team for a custom plan.
  • How long are images stored? ‚ñº
    Customer photos are automatically deleted after 72 hours for privacy and GDPR compliance. Generated try-on results are stored for 30 days for analytics purposes. You can request immediate deletion at any time.
  • Is there a webhook system? ‚ñº
    Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include webhook support. Configure webhooks in your dashboard to receive real-time notifications for try-on completions, errors, and usage milestones.
  • Can I use VMIZE on mobile apps? ‚ñº
    Yes! Our REST API works with iOS, Android, and React Native apps. Mobile SDKs are available for Professional and Enterprise plans. Contact support for SDK documentation and examples.

Account & Security

  • How do I reset my password? ‚ñº
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send a password reset link. For security, the link expires after 1 hour.
  • Is my data secure? ‚ñº
    Yes. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3), secure API keys, and follow GDPR/CCPA compliance. Customer photos are encrypted at rest and in transit, and deleted after 72 hours. We never sell your data.
  • Can I have multiple team members? ‚ñº
    Professional and Enterprise plans support team collaboration. Add team members from your dashboard with customizable permissions (Admin, Developer, or Viewer roles).
  • How do I cancel my account? ‚ñº
    You can cancel anytime from your dashboard under Settings ‚Üí Billing. Your service continues until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial months, but you keep access until the period ends.
